AI Network Intrusion Detection System

A network intrusion detection system that analyzes packet capture data and utilizes deep learning to detect port scans. It is crucial to keep any threats outside our network, port scans are only one of many signs malicous activity may occur. Using deep learning helps sift through internet traffic and detect signs of potential malicous IPs for further investigation and remediation.

Dependencies

  • Jupyter Notebooks
  • Scapy
  • Pandas
  • Sci-kit Learn
  • TensorFlow
